How to know about tense


1.Present Indefinite Tense:
S+V1/Vs/es+O.
Ex:
= I read a book.
= He reads a book.
= I have a pen.
2.Present Continuous Tense:
S+am/is/are+Ving+O.
Ex:
= I am reading a book.
=
=
3. Present Perfect Tense:
S+have/has+V3+O.
Ex:
= I have been a pen.
= He has read a book.
=A book has been read by him.
4. Present Perfect Continuous Tense:
S+have/has+been + Ving+O.
Ex:
= I have been being a pen.
= He has been reading a book.
=A book has been being read by him.
5.Past Indefinite Tense:
S+V2+O.
Ex:
= I had a pen.
= He ate rice.
=Rice was eaten by him.
6. Past Continuous Tense:
S + was/were +Ving + O

Ex:
= I was eating rice.
= He was eating rice.
= Rice was being eaten by him.
7.Past Perfect Tense:
S+had+V3+O.
Ex:
= I had been a pen.
= He had eaten rice.
=Rice had been eaten by him.
8.Past Perfect Continuous Tense:
S+had + been +Ving+O.
Ex:
= I had been being a pen.
= He had been eating rice.
=Rice had been being eaten by him.
9. Future Indefinite Tense:
S+shall/will+V1+O.
Ex:
= I will have a pen.
= He will write a letter.
= A letter will be written by him.
10. Future Continuous Tense:
S+shall/will+be+Ving+O.
Ex:
= I shall be having a pen.
= He will be doing the work.
= The work will be being done by him.
11. Future Perfect Tense:
S+shall/will+have+V3+O.
Ex:
= I shall have been a pen.
= He will have written a letter.
= A letter will have been written by him.
12. Future Perfect Continuos Tense:
S+shall/will+have + been +Ving+O.
Ex:
= I shall have been having a pen.
= He will have been writing a letter.
= A letter will have been being written by him.
